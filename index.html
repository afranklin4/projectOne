Favorite Meals

Beef Wellington

Beef Wellington, made famous most recently by Gordon Ramsay, is a beef tenderloin smothered in Duxelle of mushroom, garlic, and other herbs, wrapped in prosciutto, then wrapped in puff pastry, and baked. It is a very expensive dish to make, but so delicious.

Pulled Pork

Pulled pork is a delicious way to prepare pork. It involves smoking or grilling pork, then using a fork (or bear claws) to shred it. It is then tossed or doused in BBQ sauce and put on a bun. It often comes with coleslaw on top of the pork as well. Many places offer sides to accompany the pork, such as baked beans, coleslaw, or cornbread. A cold beer is also an excellent side dish.

Steak & Potatoes

Steak and potatoes is a classic dish. I like mine medium to medium well, though some people like it cooked differently. It is often served as a pair, though my favorite way to prepare it is with asparagus (if it's in season).
A cold beer also goes well with this meal.
